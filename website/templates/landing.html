For Magic: The Gathering Arena

A coach in your ear, every turn of the game.

mtgacoach watches your Arena games as you play and talks you through them — attacks, blocks, sequencing, the line you didn't see. Out loud, while it matters, not in a post-mortem.

Subscribe $9.99 a month, through Patreon.
What it does
Live table talk
It reads the real game state — your hand, the board, the stack, every legal action — and speaks its advice so your eyes never leave the game.
Draft picks, with receipts
Pack-by-pack pick ratings built on 17lands data, so you draft the deck the numbers like and not just the rare.
An autopilot that actually plays
Step away and it takes the wheel: lands on curve, sensible combat, priority passed on time. Come back to a game still in progress, not a concession.
Our own table, our own stakes
Coaching runs on dedicated GPU hardware we operate ourselves. No per-token metering — your subscription is the whole bill.
How it works

  1. Install the app.

    Grab the Windows installer from GitHub, or pip install mtgacoach anywhere Python runs.

  2. Add your license key.

    Subscribe on Patreon, link your account, and paste the key into the app under Settings.

  3. Queue up.

    Launch Arena, launch mtgacoach, and play. The coaching starts with your first priority.
